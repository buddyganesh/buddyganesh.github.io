Journals for the quiet, necessary work of healing

The Healing Series began with six volumes — and continues to grow. Each journal is written to meet you exactly where you are, whether you're starting with loss, silence, forgiveness, or something else entirely. There is no fixed endpoint to healing, and there is no fixed endpoint to this collection.

Each volume stands alone. Read one. Read several. Follow your own path through them at your own pace.

About the Author

Buddy Ganesh

BG
Author · Buddy Ganesh

Written from the inside of the experience, not the outside looking in

The Healing Series was not written from a clinical distance. It was written by someone who has sat in waiting rooms, watched a loved one navigate a diagnosis that changed everything, and learned — slowly, imperfectly — what it means to keep going when the path forward is unclear.

Buddy Ganesh is an independent author based in Central Florida. His writing spans memoir, guided journals, and children's literature — all rooted in the belief that the act of putting words on a page can restore what loss has taken away.

He is also the founder of Still Me Rising, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit supporting adults with epilepsy and neurological conditions through creative and expressive therapies. A portion of proceeds from The Healing Series supports that mission.

His memoir, Michael's Journey: A True Story of Epilepsy, Resilience, and the Healing Power of Calm, tells the story that ultimately gave rise to all of this work.
